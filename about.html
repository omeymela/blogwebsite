About The Style Edit

Where fashion meets storytelling — discover our story, mission, and team

Our Story

The Style Edit was born from a simple idea: fashion is more than just clothing — it's a form of self-expression, a way to tell your story without saying a word. What started as a personal style journal has grown into a vibrant community of fashion enthusiasts, industry insiders, and trendsetters.

Founded in 2020, we've been at the forefront of fashion journalism, covering everything from the runways of Paris to the streets of Lagos. We believe that style is universal, and everyone deserves access to the latest trends, timeless tips, and honest fashion advice.

Today, The Style Edit is read by thousands of fashion lovers across the globe. We're committed to sustainable fashion, diversity, and empowering our readers to embrace their unique style.

— The Style Edit Team

Our Journey

Milestones that shaped The Style Edit

2020

The Beginning

The Style Edit was founded as a personal style blog by Sarah Mitchell. The first post — "Finding Your Signature Style" — goes live.

2021

Growing the Team

Marcus Cole and Jessica Wu join as editors. The blog expands to cover runways, sustainable fashion, and street style.

2022

Community Milestone

The Style Edit reaches 100,000 monthly readers. We launch our first sustainability guide and start featuring emerging designers.

2023

Global Expansion

Amara Okafor joins as a contributor, bringing coverage from Africa. The Style Edit becomes truly global in its perspective.

2024

The Style Edit Today

We continue to grow our community, cover fashion weeks around the world, and champion sustainable, inclusive fashion.
